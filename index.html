About Royzee Weather

Our Mission

Royzee Weather is dedicated to providing accurate, reliable, and accessible weather information to people around the world. We believe that everyone deserves access to high-quality weather data to make informed decisions about their daily lives and future plans.

Our Technology

We utilize cutting-edge meteorological technology, combining data from multiple sources including satellites, weather stations, and atmospheric models. Our advanced algorithms process billions of data points to deliver precise forecasts and real-time weather information.

Real-time Data

Powered by OpenWeatherMap API, we provide up-to-date weather information for over 200,000 cities worldwide. All data is updated in real-time to ensure accuracy and reliability.

Weather Data Sources

We partner with leading meteorological organizations worldwide to ensure the accuracy and reliability of our weather data. Our sources include:

  • OpenWeatherMap API
  • National Weather Services
  • Satellite imagery providers
  • Weather radar networks
  • Atmospheric monitoring stations

Features

  • Real-time weather data
  • 5-day weather forecast
  • Hourly forecast
  • Weather maps
  • Multiple units (Celsius/Fahrenheit)
  • Location-based weather
