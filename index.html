About GlobalWeather

Our Mission

GlobalWeather is dedicated to providing accurate, reliable, and accessible weather information to people around the world. We believe that everyone deserves access to high-quality weather data to make informed decisions about their daily lives and future plans.

Our Technology

We utilize cutting-edge meteorological technology, combining data from multiple sources including satellites, weather stations, and atmospheric models. Our advanced algorithms process billions of data points to deliver precise forecasts and real-time weather information.

Our Team

Our team consists of experienced meteorologists, data scientists, and software engineers who are passionate about weather and technology. Together, we work to continuously improve our services and expand our coverage to more locations worldwide.

Weather Data Sources

We partner with leading meteorological organizations worldwide to ensure the accuracy and reliability of our weather data. Our sources include:

  • National Weather Services
  • Satellite imagery providers
  • Weather radar networks
  • Atmospheric monitoring stations

Accuracy Commitment

We are committed to providing the most accurate weather information possible. Our forecasts are continuously verified against actual weather conditions, and we transparently report our accuracy rates for different timeframes and locations.

Global Coverage

With coverage for over 200,000 cities worldwide, GlobalWeather ensures that no matter where you are, you can access reliable weather information in multiple languages and units of measurement.
